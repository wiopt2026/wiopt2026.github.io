Registration

Registration details:

Regular

  • Main event Member (IEEE-IFIP) / No member: 8300/ 8850 SEK (750/800 Euros*)
  • Main event + Workshops (IEEE-IFIP) / No member: 8850/9400 SEK (800/850 Euros*)
  • Workshop (IEEE-IFIP) / No member: 4425/4975 SEK (400/450 Euros*)

Student only (not applicable for author registration)

  • Main event + Workshops (IEEE-IFIP) / No member: 4975/5525 (450/500 Euros*)
  • Workshop (IEEE-IFIP) / No member: 2763/3313 SEK (250/300 Euros*)

Extra banquet ticket 1400SEK (129 Euros*)
Late registration +1100 SEK (100 Euros*)

Authors must choose either Option 1 or Option 2, depending on whether they wish to attend the workshops or need to cover papers in both the main event and the workshops. Each registration can cover up to two papers—either two papers in the main event or one paper in the main event and one in the workshops. A workshop-only registration covers one paper presented in the workshops.

Note that workshop only registration do not include the banquet.

Online registration will be available until the 15th of May.
that if a registration is cancelled it will be reimbursed 50% of the fee until the 1st of May.

The link for the registration can be found in:

  • IEEE/IFIP Member: https://www.trippus.net/WiOpt2025_IEEE_IFIP_Member
  • Non-member: https://www.trippus.net/WiOpt2025_Non-member
  • [Student] IEEE/IFIP Member: https://www.trippus.net/WiOpt2025_Student_member
  • [Student] Non-member: https://www.trippus.net/WiOpt2025_Student_non-member

Please note that the payment is made in Swedish Krona (SEK), and the exact amount in Euros (EUR) will vary according to the prevailing exchange rate on the day of the transaction.

Inquiries regarding the payment can be sent to liu@travelteam.se
