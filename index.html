WELCOME to WiOpt 2026

WELCOME to the website of WiOpt 2026, the 23rd International Symposium on Modeling and Optimization in Mobile, Ad hoc, and Wireless Networks. WiOpt 2026 will showcase state-of-the-art research contributions from different fields in networks. It welcomes original and high-quality submissions related to modeling, performance evaluation, and optimization of networks. The focus includes but is not limited to mobile networks, edge computing, intelligent networks, content-driven networks, cognitive networks, vehicular networks, robotic systems, Internet.

WiOpt 2026 will be hosted in Columbus, Ohio, USA.

The conference will take place at The Ohio State University, Columbus (main campus).
